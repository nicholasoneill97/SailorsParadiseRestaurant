About Us

Founder, Nicholas O'Neill, has always dreamed of starting a business with his wife, Nicole. They specifically envisioned a day where they could open an italian restaurant together. The two things that Nick and Nicole love the most are cooking and the ocean. This restaurant represents exactly what they both love the most in this world.
